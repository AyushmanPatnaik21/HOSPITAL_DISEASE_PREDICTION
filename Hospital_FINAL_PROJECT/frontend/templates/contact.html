Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What Are Your Operating Hours?

Our hospital operates 24/7 with round-the-clock emergency services. Regular OPD timings are 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Saturday.

How Do I Book an Appointment?

You can book appointments through our online portal, mobile app, or by calling our reception desk. We offer both in-person and telemedicine consultations.

What Payment Methods Do You Accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, and insurance claims. Payment plans are available for major procedures.

Is My Data Secure?

Yes, we employ enterprise-grade encryption and comply with all healthcare data protection regulations to ensure your privacy.

Do You Have Parking Facilities?

We provide ample parking for patients and visitors. Wheelchair accessible spaces are available near the main entrance.

Do You Offer Emergency Services?

Yes, our emergency department is fully equipped and staffed 24/7 with highly trained medical professionals for all emergencies.
